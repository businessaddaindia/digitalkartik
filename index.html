Inspired by senge's philosophy, a group of young corporate professionals came together in 2019 to set up smile Foundation to work with grassoots initiatives for effecting positive changes in the life of underprivileged children, their families and communities.

Vission

Work as a catalyst in bringing sustanable change in the lives of underprivileged childrenn, youth and women, with a life-cycle approach of development.
